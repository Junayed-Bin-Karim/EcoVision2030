Event Overview

EcoVision 2030 is a global innovation challenge inviting bright minds to develop sustainable solutions for environmental challenges. This year's theme focuses on circular economy and waste reduction strategies.

The competition consists of two rounds with expert judging panels from academia and industry. Finalists will present at our international sustainability conference.

About EcoVision 2030

EcoVision was established in 2015 as a platform for environmental innovation. The 2030 edition marks our commitment to the UN Sustainable Development Goals, with special focus on Goals 11, 12, and 13.

"Your Ideas, Our Future" reflects our belief that collective creativity can solve the planet's most pressing ecological challenges through technology, design, and community engagement.
